sort1 uses: Bubble sort

How do you know?: It was the fastest on the sorted array, which conforms to omega n.

sort2 uses: Merge sort

How do you know?: It was the fastest on the unsorted array, with n log n being quicker than n^2. It also had roughly the same time to sort the unsorted and sorted array, n log n for both.

sort3 uses: Selection sort

How do you know?: Process of deduction. It was also the slowest on the sorted array, with an omega of n^2.
